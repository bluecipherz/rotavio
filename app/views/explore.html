SOFTWARE
Helicopters require sophisticated simulation models for analysis. Rotavio Labs is actively developing various simulation models that are useful for helicopter design and analysis. Contact us to consult for helicopter design and analysis
RLDyn

The main source of vibration for a helicopter is its Main Rotor. RLDyn is a structural dynamic analysis program for rotor blades. It helps understand the blade properties and design it for minimum vibrations.

RLCar

RLCar is a comprehensive aeroelastic and flight dynamics simulation program that will be useful to solve helicopters general maneuver problem. It predicts rotor craft loads by solving highly nonlinear aeroelastic responses. RLCar is useful in calculating rotorcraft performance, trim, blade motion & airloading, structural loads, vibration, noise, aeroelastic stability, and flight dynamics.

RLCon (under development)

RLCon is a Helicopter Control systems simulation software. The software uses an in-house developed model of the helicopter as plant. It is used to observe the closed loop position hold performance by gain tuning in software. This helps reduce the time required for gain tuning in actual flight of the helicopter
